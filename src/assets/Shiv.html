‘Succession’ Season 3 Returns, And So Do Shiv Roy’s Power Pants

In Season 2 of Succession, the mogul-in-the-making adopts a very specific form of power dressing—one built around tailored, wide-leg trousers. Ahead of the HBO show’s Sunday return, here's a look at how to emulate the character’s style.

By Sadie Stein
Updated Oct. 25, 2021
Tap story

In Season 1 of Succession, Shiv (Sarah Snook) adhered to softly hued workwear (often Theory) and Veronica Lake-esque hair that provided a visual counterpoint to other characters’ hard edges.

Peter Kramer/HBO, Cover: HBO

When she changes course for shark-infested corporate waters, her style adapts to meet the mark. Shiv's pants in particular boast a hyper-high waist, voluminous swish around the calf and a length that begs for heels.

Macall B. Polay/HBO
“They’re menswear-inspired in that Old Hollywood way, but feminine.”

—Succession costume designer Michelle Matland

Peter Kramer/HBO

“Shiv Roy’s Pants Should Take Over Waystar,” ran a headline on fashion site the Cut. Meanwhile, fans wondered how—or if—they could emulate Shiv’s trouser style.

Peter Kramer/HBO

Ms. Matland advised balancing the trousers’ statement with a simple top, like the character’s turtlenecks, tucked-in button-up shirts and trim jackets.
